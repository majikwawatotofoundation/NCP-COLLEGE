Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking with Nova Rides

What do I need to rent a car with Nova Rides?

Just a valid ID or passport, a valid driver’s license (international permits accepted), and a working phone number. That’s it — we keep it simple!

Do you offer airport pick-up and drop-off?

Yes! We regularly pick up and drop off at Moi International Airport (MBA), Ukunda Airstrip, and most hotels in Mombasa, Nyali, Diani, and Bamburi. Just let us know your flight details.

Is a security deposit required?

Yes, a refundable security deposit applies depending on the vehicle. It’s fully returned within 24 hours after the car is checked and returned in good condition.

Are your cars fully insured?

Absolutely. Every vehicle comes with comprehensive insurance. We also offer optional extra coverage for complete peace of mind.

Can I hire a car with a driver?

Yes, and it’s very popular! Many guests prefer a friendly local driver who knows the coast inside out. Just select “with driver” during booking or let us know.

What is your fuel policy?

We operate on a full-to-full policy — you receive the car with a full tank and return it full. No surprises, no extra charges.
