About the College

Building Practical Skills for Real Careers

North Coast Prime College of Technology & Business Studies is a post-secondary training institution offering hands-on, career-focused programs designed to meet today’s workforce demands.

Our approach emphasizes practical learning, flexible study options, and industry-relevant skills across technology, business, hospitality, cosmetology, engineering trades, and languages.

We serve school leavers, working professionals, and adult learners seeking employable skills, entrepreneurship opportunities, or pathways to further education.

Our Mission

To deliver practical, affordable, and market-driven education that empowers learners with real-world skills.

Our Vision

To be a leading skills-based training institution along the Kenyan coast and beyond.
