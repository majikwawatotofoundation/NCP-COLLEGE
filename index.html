Why Choose North Coast Prime College

At North Coast Prime College, we focus on practical, career-ready education that prepares students for today’s job market.

Our programs are designed with industry relevance in mind, delivered by experienced trainers, and supported by recognized examination bodies.

Whether you are starting your career or upgrading your skills, we provide a supportive learning environment that helps you succeed.

  • Practical, skill-based training
  • Flexible day & evening classes
  • Recognized examination bodies (KNEC, NITA, City & Guilds)
  • Affordable fees & flexible payment options
  • Convenient Mtwapa location
  • Internship & industrial attachment support
